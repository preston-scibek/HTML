HOW TO MAKE PB&J

BY PRESTON SCIBEK

INGREDIENTS

  • BREAD
  • JELLY
  • PEANUT BUTTER
  1. Get the ingredients out of the fridge.
  2. Open the jars and separate the bread.
  3. Get a knife to spread the butter and jelly.
  4. Spread the Peanut Butter on one half and the jelly on the other half.
  5. Combine the two halves together and put the knife in the sink.
  6. Put the jars away and enjoy.

This recipe is to allow all those who want to make PB&J sandwiches and cant to make them.
